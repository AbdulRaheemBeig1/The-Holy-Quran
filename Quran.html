Order of Surah Order of Juz

Dear Noble Soul

They are the most fortunate who receive Blessings and Guidance from the Almighty.

Holy Qur'an is the true word of Almighty. Qur'an is the message for the entire humanity. No single community or group has exclusive rights for possessing it. It is for one and all.

To make life most prosperous and successful by seeking guidance from Holy Qur'an, it is advisable to follow certain etiquettes.

  1. One must read with the intention to seek peace and guidance.
  2. The person and the place should be physically clean.
  3. Keep the Holy Qur'an on the top of all books. Nothing should be kept on the top of Qur'an.
  4. One should not touch the Holy Qur'an in an inebriated condition. Do not touch the Holy Qur'an after consuming alcohol or any form of it.
